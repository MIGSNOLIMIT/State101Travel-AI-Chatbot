# Program Details & Policies

## Overview
All the detailed program information (fees, training structure, payment options, hidden charges, orientation specifics) is discussed during the initial briefing and assessment at our office.

## Qualifications
- Open to applicants with or without prior training or experience.
- Applicants must be willing to undergo training and develop necessary skills.

## Age Policy
- No age limit, provided the applicant is physically capable of performing required tasks.

## Gender Policy
- Open to all genders.

## Education
- Accepts both graduates and undergraduates.

## Orientation
- Orientation is mandatory so applicants are fully prepared and understand the process.
- Rescheduling must be requested via official contact numbers.

## Legitimacy
- Company is officially registered and has a permit to operate issued by the Municipality of Pasig.

## Contact & Visit Recommendation
- Appointment booking is recommended to ensure smooth processing and timely assistance.
